Is Your Skin Thick Enough for the Truth?

In a London where blasphemy laws have been reintroduced, a scientist discovers a secret in human DNA that could cost him his life. When he is forced onto a project and confronted with DMT – the most powerful hallucinogen known to science, things become a little strange.
